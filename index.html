Lunar Odyssey

As part of the small Fractal Blue team, a small Visual Purple division, I helped in the creation and shipping of a small VR simulation experience depicting the steps of the Apollo-era Lunar Module launch. I implemented critical interactables that were required for the quest system. Also, I took part in the launch research, using real NASA documentation to make the quest system be as accurate as possible. Additionally, I helped debug major bugs before the release date and I was responsible for learning the Steamworks tools to publish the game.

About Me

I am an experienced Unreal Engine 4/5 c++ programmer currently living in Meridian, Idaho. I like to bring ideas to life using the tools provided by the engine. I use the standards and conventions provided by Epic Games to create dynamic data-driven gameplay systems. I acquired my Bachelor's degree in Computer Science and a Minor in Cybersecurity at Central Connecticut State University and decided to specialize in game development because of my passion for games. My favorite games are Halo Infinity, Halo MCC, Age of Empires 4, Resident Evil 4, Silent Hill 4, and the Tomb Raider games. I also like 3D modeling and animation so I started learning Blender in my free time. In my free time, I like to watch anime and my favorite series are Attack on Titan, Berserk, Tokyo Ghoul, Re:Zero, and many more.
